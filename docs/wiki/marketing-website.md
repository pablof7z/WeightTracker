# Marketing Website

## Deployment & Links

The marketing website is deployed to cut.f7z.io. It links to the TestFlight URL at https://testflight.apple.com/join/muNV9QP5. The site includes 4 TestFlight CTAs, all wired to this join URL. <!-- [^e2ed7-7] -->

## Voice & Copy

The marketing website uses a non-needy, editorial/brutalist voice (Ben Settle / Basecamp-manifesto style), not a generic SaaS funnel tone. The copy is trimmed compared to its initial version: all four redundant screenshot captions are removed, the duplicate closing paragraph in the deficit section is removed, and every body paragraph is tightened while keeping the voice and punchy lists intact. <!-- [^e2ed7-8] -->

## Visual Design

The marketing website's palette is pulled from the app itself: black ink, iOS green, and muted 'worst' red, using SF Pro for Apple visitors with Inter/Archivo fallback. <!-- [^e2ed7-9] -->

## Interactions & Animation

All scroll-triggered animations have been removed from the marketing website, including fade/slide-in reveals, the count-up animation on the deficit number, the strikethrough draw-on animation, parallax effects, the sticky manifesto headline, and the floating/tilted hero phone.

# Minichart Data Refresh

## Data Refresh

Minichart data refreshes after weigh-in without requiring a restart, including animation. The chart updates immediately to reflect a newly entered weight without requiring an app restart. When a new weight reading is saved, inCutReadings and projection are recomputed so that the chart renders the new data immediately. The chart line animates smoothly (ease-in-out over 0.4 seconds) when a new reading is added, rather than updating instantly or with complex animation. The weight log data view is located on the Progress screen (not the Today screen).
